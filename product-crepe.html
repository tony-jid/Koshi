Duck Crepes

Sushi is a representative food of Japan that dates back at least 1000 years. In its most common form it is a finger of vinegared rice garnished with either sliced raw fish or non-seafood garnish such as cooked egg. This form of sushi is known as nigiri.
Another popular form of sushi is norimaki (seaweed roll) which consists of narrow strips of seafood and/or vegetables arranged on a layer of vinegared rice. The layer or sheet of rice is spread on a sheet of nori (toasted seaweed) and rolled. The roll is cut into rounds (nori roll) convenient for serving.
