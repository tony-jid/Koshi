Food Service

Our food service offer has been specifically developed to support the needs of restaurants, hotels, catering companies, and other businesses whose customers are demanding fresh and tasty products, which they can’t make in-house or do not have the time to make themselves.

We have developed a range of products specifically for resale in retail outlets. Our products are can be found in supermarkets, convenience stores, cafes, bars and major events. Being “retail ready” all our retail packs are labelled with all the information meeting labelling laws, and are barcode compliant.
